Solo Leveling

Introduction

Solo Leveling is a South Korean fantasy web novel and manhwa written by Chugong. It follows Sung Jin-Woo, the world's weakest hunter, who gains a mysterious System that allows him to level up and become stronger after every battle.

Story Overview

Sung Jin-Woo begins his journey as the weakest hunter alive, barely surviving low-level dungeons. After surviving the Double Dungeon, he awakens the System and begins his rise to become the Shadow Monarch.

Signature Abilities

  • Shadow Extraction
  • Shadow Storage
  • Stealth
  • Dagger Mastery
  • Quick Recovery
  • Sprint
  • Ruler's Authority
  • Shadow Exchange

Shadow Monarch Powers

After inheriting Ashborn's power, Jin-Woo becomes the Shadow Monarch and commands an endless army of shadows to protect humanity.
