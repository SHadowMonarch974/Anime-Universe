INTRODUCTION

Attack on Titan (Japanese: Shingeki no Kyojin) is one of the most influential anime and manga series of the 21st century. Created by Hajime Isayama, it debuted as a manga in 2009, while the anime adaptation began in 2013.

The story takes place in a world where humanity survives behind enormous walls to protect itself from giant humanoid creatures known as Titans.

BACKGROUND OF THE STORY

For over a century, humans have lived inside three concentric walls:

  • Wall Maria
  • Wall Rose
  • Wall Sina

The people believe these walls are humanity's only protection against the Titans, mysterious giants that devour humans.

Peace is shattered when the Colossal Titan breaches the outer wall, allowing Titans to invade and destroy entire cities.

MAIN CHARACTERS

1. EREN YEAGER

  • The protagonist of the series.
  • Witnessed the death of his mother.
  • Swore to destroy every Titan.
  • Inherited the Attack Titan.

2. MIKASA ACKERMAN

  • Eren's adoptive sister.
  • One of humanity's strongest soldiers.
  • Exceptionally skilled with ODM Gear.
  • Fiercely loyal to Eren.

3. ARMIN ARLERT

  • Eren's childhood friend.
  • Known for his brilliant strategies.
  • Always thinks before fighting.
  • Later inherits the Colossal Titan.

4. LEVI ACKERMAN

  • Captain of the Scout Regiment.
  • Known as Humanity's Strongest Soldier.
  • Master of ODM combat.
  • Respected throughout the military.

5. ERWIN SMITH

  • Commander of the Scout Regiment.
  • A brilliant military strategist.
  • Inspired soldiers to sacrifice everything.
  • Dedicated his life to uncovering the truth.

TITANS

Titans are giant humanoid creatures ranging from only a few meters to over sixty meters in height. Most Titans possess little or no intelligence and exist only to hunt and devour humans.

TYPES OF TITANS

PURE TITANS

  • Ordinary Titans with almost no intelligence.
  • Wander without purpose.
  • Attack humans instinctively.

THE NINE TITANS

Nine unique Titan powers exist, each possessing extraordinary abilities passed down through inheritance.

  • Attack Titan
  • Founding Titan
  • Colossal Titan
  • Armored Titan
  • Female Titan
  • Beast Titan
  • War Hammer Titan
  • Jaw Titan
  • Cart Titan

MILITARY DIVISIONS

SCOUT REGIMENT

  • Explores beyond the Walls.
  • Fights Titans directly.
  • Includes Levi, Erwin, Eren, Mikasa and Armin.

GARRISON REGIMENT

  • Protects cities and defensive walls.
  • Responsible for civilian safety.

MILITARY POLICE

  • Protects the Royal Government.
  • Operates mainly inside Wall Sina.

SURVIVAL

WAR & CONFLICT

TRUTH & DECEPTION

  • The search for truth changes the fate of humanity.

STORY THEMES

WAR & CONFLICT

  • The series explores hatred and revenge across generations.
  • Every battle comes with sacrifice and moral consequences.

TRUTH & DECEPTION

  • Humanity lives surrounded by hidden secrets.
  • The mystery of the Titans changes the fate of the world.

MORALITY

  • The story rarely has absolute heroes or villains.
  • Every side believes it is fighting for justice.

STORY DEVELOPMENT

SEASON 1

  • Fall of Wall Maria.
  • Eren joins the military.
  • Discovery of the Attack Titan.
  • Battle against the Female Titan.

SEASON 2

  • Reveals major Titan mysteries.
  • Introduces the Beast Titan.
  • Shocking betrayals change everything.

SEASON 3

  • Political conspiracies inside the Walls.
  • Secrets hidden in Eren's basement.
  • The truth about the outside world.

FINAL SEASON

  • The conflict expands beyond the Walls.
  • Marley and Eldia enter full-scale war.
  • Eren begins the Rumbling.

SYMBOLISM

THE WALLS REPRESENT

  • Fear.
  • Isolation.
  • Government control.
  • Humanity's limited understanding of reality.

TITANS REPRESENT

  • Existential threats.
  • Human violence.
  • The endless cycle of hatred.

IMPACT & POPULARITY

  • Exceptional storytelling.
  • Memorable characters.
  • Outstanding animation.
  • Deep political and philosophical themes.
  • Unexpected plot twists.

Attack on Titan became one of the most influential anime series ever created and remains a global phenomenon.

CONCLUSION

Attack on Titan is more than an action anime. It is a powerful story about freedom, sacrifice, war, revenge and the human desire to fight against fate. Through unforgettable characters and incredible world-building, it has become one of the greatest anime of all time.
